Frequently Asked Questions

How much commission can I earn?

30-50% of your referrals' trading fees, paid in USDT. Your level depends on monthly volume and active traders. New affiliates get a 5-month rate protection period.

When do I get paid?

Commissions are settled within 1-2 hours after each trade. Paid directly to your OKX account in USDT. No minimum payout threshold.

What do I need to apply?

Your OKX UID (KYC verified account), your main channel/community link, and your Telegram contact. The application takes about 2 minutes.

How long until approval?

1-2 business days. Joshua reviews each application personally. You'll receive an approval notification in this Telegram chat, along with your referral link and marketing materials.

What happens after approval?

You'll receive: your personal referral link, marketing banners, copy-paste intro templates, and a direct line to your BD manager. Start sharing immediately.

Can I build a sub-affiliate network?

Yes. You can recruit sub-affiliates under your account. Set custom commission splits. Earn from your network's referrals in a multi-tier structure.

⚠️ What's not allowed?

Impersonating OKX official accounts, bidding on OKX brand keywords in search ads, or using look-alike URLs. Violations result in immediate partner disqualification.
